Emedo Hotel

The Emedo Hotel is located at old airport, just a 10-minute walk from the National library. Built in 1997, it had old school charm and many rooms featured high, crown-molded ceilings. 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern day scratch and guestrooms comes equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay.
